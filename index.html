MAXsi POS for Minimarkets & Grocery Stores

MAXsi POS is an Android-based Point of Sale (POS) application built for minimarkets and grocery stores that need fast transactions, reliable inventory control, and clear business reports—without complex setup.

Category: Point of Sale, Retail Management, Inventory
Platform: Android
Target users: Minimarkets, grocery stores, retail SMEs
License: MIT
Official Website GitHub Repository

Why MAXsi POS for Minimarkets?

Minimarkets and grocery stores handle many fast-moving items and frequent transactions. MAXsi POS helps you keep sales and stock data organized, reduce manual errors, and make daily operations easier for cashiers and store owners.

  • Speed up checkout with simple transaction flow
  • Keep product and stock data in one place
  • Review daily and monthly sales summaries
  • Understand performance using basic profit loss reports

Key Features

  • Cash and cashless sales transactions
  • Product and inventory management
  • Stock monitoring and control
  • Sales reports and profit & loss summaries
  • Daily and monthly transaction recaps
  • Transaction history tracking
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Optimized for small and medium retail businesses
  • Lightweight and stable for daily operations

Use Cases

  • Minimarkets and neighborhood grocery stores
  • Small retail shops with fast-moving items
  • Stores that need basic inventory tracking
  • Retail SMEs looking for a simple Android POS

System Requirements

  • Android 8.0 (Oreo) or newer
  • Minimum 2 GB RAM
  • Internet connection for data synchronization

Installation

  1. Download the MAXsi POS APK file.
  2. Enable Install from Unknown Sources on your Android device.
  3. Install the APK and follow the setup instructions.
  4. Launch the app and configure your store data.
