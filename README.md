MAXsi POS is an Android-based Point of Sale (POS) application designed for small and medium retail businesses.  
It helps store owners manage sales transactions, inventory, and basic financial reports in a simple and reliable way.

This project focuses on practical retail workflows and ease of use, making it suitable for grocery stores, convenience shops, mobile phone counters, minimarkets, and other SME-scale retail businesses.

---

## Key Features

- Cash and cashless sales transactions  
- Product and inventory management  
- Stock monitoring and control  
- Sales reports and profit & loss summaries  
- Daily and monthly transaction recaps  
- Transaction history tracking  
- Simple and user-friendly interface  
- Optimized for small and medium retail businesses (SMEs)  
- Lightweight and stable for daily operations  

---

## Target Users

- Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)  
- Retail store owners  
- Grocery stores and minimarkets  
- Mobile phone shops and service counters  
- Cashiers and store administrators  

---

## Platform

- Android

---

## System Requirements

- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or newer  
- Minimum 2 GB RAM  
- Internet connection for data synchronization  

---

## Installation

1. Download the MAXsi POS APK file.  
2. Enable **Install from Unknown Sources** on your Android device.  
3. Install the APK and follow the on-screen instructions.  
4. Launch the app and complete the initial setup.
